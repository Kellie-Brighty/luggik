### Update: 6/28/2026, 12:15:01 PM
### Luggik Project Vision & Flow
Luggik is an escrow and logistics platform connecting Buyers (senders) and Runners (logistics partners) to ensure safe, trustless package delivery.

**Core Flow:**
1. **Runner KYC**: Runners sign up (Firebase Auth) and complete Business KYC via Dojah. Webhooks handle approval and welcome emails.
2. **Escrow Funding**: Buyers create Errands and fund an escrow wallet to guarantee payment.
3. **Delivery & Tracking**: Runners accept errands, pick up items, and update status. Buyers track progress.
4. **Payout**: Upon successful delivery confirmation by the buyer, funds are released to the Runner. Disputes trigger an admin review flow.

### Update: 6/28/2026, 12:21:13 PM
### Correction to Luggik Project Vision & Flow
Luggik is a Logistics-based Escrow platform designed to eradicate e-commerce disputes by utilizing government-verified logistics companies as the trusted middlemen between a Buyer and an untrusted Vendor.

**The Actual Errand Flow:**
1. **Escrow Creation**: A buyer wants to purchase an item from an untrusted vendor. They create an escrow on Luggik and request a verified logistics partner (Runner).
2. **Pre-Pickup Verification**: The Rider goes to the Vendor's location. **Crucially, before taking the item**, the Rider takes pictures of the item and sends them to the Buyer through the app.
3. **Buyer Decision**:
   - **Approved**: Buyer confirms the item looks correct. Rider picks it up, delivers it to the Buyer. Escrow funds are released and split (Vendor gets paid, Rider gets paid).
   - **Rejected**: Buyer rejects the item based on the pictures. The Rider cancels the order. The item never leaves the Vendor. The Buyer does NOT pay the Vendor. The Rider is compensated for the attempted pickup, and the remaining funds are refunded to the Buyer.

This unique flow prevents disputes by ensuring buyer satisfaction *before* the item even enters transit.
